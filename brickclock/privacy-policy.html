隐私政策

最后更新：2026年3月2日

数据收集

搬砖闹钟不会收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。所有闹钟数据和设置均存储在您的设备本地。

网络请求

本应用当前版本不发起任何网络请求。节假日数据内置于应用中，随应用版本更新。

通知权限

本应用需要通知权限以在指定时间提醒您。通知内容仅包含您设置的闹钟标签，不包含任何敏感信息。

数据存储

闹钟设置和应用偏好存储在设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

联系方式

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系我们。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Data Collection

BrickClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All alarm data and settings are stored locally on your device.

Network Requests

The current version of this app does not make any network requests. Holiday data is bundled within the app and updated with app releases.

Notification Permission

This app requires notification permission to remind you at scheduled times. Notification content only includes your alarm labels and contains no sensitive information.

Data Storage

Alarm settings and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
