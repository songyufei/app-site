Privacy Policy

Sewp - Screenshot Stitching & Editing

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Sewp ("we", "our", or "us") is a screenshot stitching and editing application. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Our Core Principle

Sewp is designed to process images entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data or images on external servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your photos or images
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Personal information of any kind

3. Photo Library Access

Sewp requests access to your photo library solely to:

  • Read: Let you select images for stitching and editing.
  • Write: Save edited and stitched images back to your photo library.

Your photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

4. Screen Recording (Broadcast Extension)

Sewp includes a Broadcast Upload Extension for scroll capture functionality. Screen recording frames are processed locally on your device to create long screenshots. No screen recording data is transmitted externally.

5. In-App Purchases

Sewp offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Sewp Pro") that unlocks additional features. All purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase management is governed by Apple's Terms and Conditions.

6. Web Snapshot Feature

Sewp includes a web snapshot feature that loads web pages you specify to capture screenshots. The URLs you enter are used solely to render the web page on your device. We do not log, store, or transmit the URLs you visit.

7. Share Extension

Sewp includes a Share Extension that allows you to send images from other apps to Sewp for processing. Shared images are stored temporarily in a local App Group container and are cleaned up after processing. No data is transmitted externally.

8. Third-Party Services

Sewp does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party service involved is Apple's infrastructure (App Store for purchases), which is governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

9. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • User preferences: Stored locally via UserDefaults.
  • Project history: Stored locally via SwiftData.
  • Temporary files: Broadcast frames and shared images are stored temporarily in the App Group container and cleaned up after processing.

10. Children's Privacy

Sewp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
